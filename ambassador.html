Become a Community Ambassador

For the past few years, we (Edo and Amalia) have been building Climatech Jobs Israel as an unpaid side project alongside our day jobs.

Today, the community connects thousands of people across Israel's climate ecosystem, but we have much bigger ambitions than we can build on our own.

We're looking for a small team of volunteer Community Ambassadors who want to help us take the community to the next level.

What we're trying to build

Our goal is simple:

Become the first place climate-focused companies think of when they're looking for talent in Israel.
— and at the same time —
Build a community where people can connect, learn, share opportunities, and help each other grow throughout their careers, regardless of where they work.

There are plenty of communities supporting businesses (Planetech, Startup Nation, etc). Our vision is to be the local support for individuals to network, learn and find jobs.

How you can contribute

We're looking for proactive people who want to take ownership of an area and build experience in something they're excited about. This could include:

  • Organizing online or in person events
  • Growing the community and connecting members
  • Social media and content
  • Sharing climate expertise
  • Improving our website or automations
  • Helping to build a funding strategy
  • Partnerships and outreach
  • Bringing completely new ideas (we're all ears)

Who we're looking for

People who:

  • Get things done without needing to be managed
  • Like taking ownership
  • Bring ideas, not just execution
  • Can commit to one ongoing area of contribution
  • Enjoy working as part of a team

How it will work

We'll meet regularly as a small team to share progress, brainstorm ideas, and help each other move things forward.

What you'll get

We're realistic. This isn't a paid position (we're not paid either).

But if you're looking to build experience, expand your network, and play an active role in Israel's climate ecosystem, we think this is a great opportunity.

You'll have the freedom to lead initiatives, try ideas, work under an established community brand, and collaborate closely with us to build something with real impact.
